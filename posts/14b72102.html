From retrovirus, to retrotransposon, to lentivirus

(I just want to organize the knowledge to a easily-memorized network).

The genome structure of HIV-1

The retrovirus life cycle from GENES XII

A typical retrovirus has three genes: gag, pol and env. Each of them translated to a long protein and then are processed by proteases to give multiple proteins.

All the retrovirus genome only has one typical transcription unite. However ,it can translate three proteins. The gag-pol are translated from the “full-length” transcript of the virus genome. Translation of pol requires a frameshift by the ribosome. In some species, gag and pol are in same open reading frames, suppression by a glutamyl-tRNA that recognizes the termination codon allows a single protein to be generated.

One thing to be noticed, the readthrough efficiency of ribosome frameshift is only 5%, which causes 20-fold outnumbers of Gag protein in comparison of Gag-Pol protein.

Env transcripts are generated by splicing.

All of the three are polyproteins.

With regard to the function, the gag gene gives rise to the proteins components of the nucleoprotein core of the virion. The pol gene encodes proteins with functions in nucleic acid synthesis and recombination. The env gene encodes components of the envelope of the particle, which also sequesters components from the cellular cytoplasmic membrane.

Geneprotein products
GagMA=matrix (between nucleocapsid and viral envelope); CA=capsid (major structural component); NC=nucleocapsid (packaging the dimer of RNA)
PolPR=protease (cleaves Gag-Pol and Env); RT=reverse transcriptase (synthesis DNA); IN=integrase (integrates provirus DNA into genome)
EnvSU=surface protein (spikes on virion interact with host); TM=transmembrane (mediates virus-host fusion)

逆转录病毒的核酸结构

R是direct repeat，也就是两个R的方向是一致的。RNA form可以看做病毒侵染细胞后的初始状态，可以看到相比于复制后的完整DNA双链，左右两边缺少了U3和U5，这是因为转录是从U5上开始，U3上的polyA signal结束。

逆转录病毒的逆转录

这个过程包括两次“跳跃”事件。

最开始用的引物是来自宿主的tRNA (a sequence of 18 bases at the 3’ end of the tRNA is base paired to a site 100 to 200 bases from 5’ end of one of the viral RNA molecules.)，这段结合tRNA的序列成为primer binding site(PBS)。注意模板是正义链，所以引物结合后是往5’那边转录的。转录到头后，逆转录酶的RNase H活性消化杂交链中的RNA，这时发生第一次跳跃，合成的这段DNA跳到3’端（这里画的是跳到另一条病毒RNA的3’端），依靠R序列结合模板。然后延伸到5’的R之后，RNaseH活性消化RNA，利用消化剩下的RNA片段作为pimer，开始cDNA第二链的合成，这次是合成正义链了，但还是先从合成了下游部分，然后发生第二次“跳跃”，跳到5’端结合上R，完成整个第二链的合成。可以看到，经过这样包含了两次跳跃后，合成的双链DNA两端就加上了完整的(U3-R-U5)组成的long terminal repeats (LTR)，这两个LTR也是direct repeats，方向完全一致。

逆转录病毒向宿主基因组的整合

这个过程需要整合酶（Integrase is the only viral protein required for the integration reaction）。现在病毒双链DNA上切2nt，再识别基因组上的target DNA（不同病毒识别序列不同）切出一个4nt的粘性末端，然后单链连接，注意这个过程没有碱基互补配对，病毒双链末端残留的2个单个儿的脱氧核苷酸会removed. 所以，整合的结果是最左边和最右边各自丢掉了2bp，不过问题不大，因为从基因组上转录出来的时候，RNA上是不包括左边的U3和最右边的U5的。

The lentivirus, protocol and advantages

先来学习名词：

“Lentivirus is a genus of retroviruses that cause chronic and deadly diseases characterized by long incubation periods, in humans and other mammalian species.[1] The genus includes the human immunodeficiency virus (HIV), which causes AIDS. Lentiviruses are distributed worldwide, and are known to be hosted in apes, cows, goats, horses, cats, and sheep as well as several other mammals.”

可以看到，lentivirus是一个“属”级分类，HIV就属于lentivirus.

用viral vector来进行分子遗传操作也不只是lentivirus，不同的病毒有一些共同的优点，如安全、低细胞毒性、稳定、细胞特异性、包装片段大、转导阳性率极高等。缺点的话，逆转录病毒整合入基因组是识别target sequence的，这个target sequence可以很短，意味着整合再基因组上位点有千千万万，保不准造成某个基因突变，或者把我们的基因转进了染色质的基因沉默区等。不过，我们可以用GFP等marker来观察表达情况。

Lentivectors Toolbox ‒ LVG ‐ EPFL

基本原理是把完整逆转录病毒的必要组分分割到不同质粒中，由于病毒基因组缺陷，无法自主复制，但可以进行整合。用两个辅助质粒生产病毒所需的蛋白，一个核心质粒中包含我们的目的片段。这3个质粒一块转进HEK293T细胞中，就生产出了复制缺陷的病毒，它的基因组中包含着我们的目的片段。当用这个病毒感染目的细胞后，就可以通过逆转录整合进去啦！
