英语杂七杂八

Created2022-09-13|Updated2022-10-18|学英语
|Word count:609|Reading time:2min|Post View:|Comments:
  1. breakfast 早饭； fast表示饥饿的状态
  2. Homeostasis 稳态；Metastasis 癌扩散
  3. totipotent 全能性的, pluripotent 多能性的
  4. Blastule 囊胚（从受精卵到形成原肠胚前，a cell ball）
  5. Hypotonic 低渗的，hypertonic 高渗的
  6. In previous single-cell mRNA-sequencing studies, the data suffered
    from a pronounced (显著的) 3′-end bias that limited analysis across full-length
    transcripts.
  7. Yes, Tumors developed within 21 days at 100% frequency (5/5) in nude mice inoculated subcutaneously with 107 cells. 这句话是在卖NCCIT细胞系的公司网站上抄的。意思是“有致瘤性，在裸鼠皮下注射107个细胞，21天后，肿瘤出现的频率是100%（做了5只裸鼠，5只裸鼠都产生了肿瘤）。裸鼠（nude mice），皮下注射（inoculated subcutaneously）
  8. diabetic mouse models 糖尿病
  9. Informed consent 知情同意
  10. 前(front)-后(back)，背(dosal)-腹(ventral/belly)，左(sinister)-右(right)，头(head/roster/anterial)-尾(tail/caudal/posterial)，对称(symmetry)-不对称(asymmetry)，左旋(S)-右旋(R)
  11. 脊椎动物(vertebrate)，无脊椎动物(invertebrate)，脊椎(vertebrae)
  12. 头(cephalus)，头孢(cephalosporin)，无脑儿(anencephalus)，颅(cranium)
  13. 外胚层(ectoderm)-中胚层(mesoderm)-内胚层(entoderm/endoderm)
  14. 打印的时候的选项，竖向的(portrait)，横向的(landscape)
  15. 有丝分裂mitosis，它的复数是mitoses. “However, cells transformed by
    RSV maintained stable properties through many mitoses.”
  16. However, the notion of Mendelian transmission of integrated genomes of RNA tumor viruses in the germ-line of healthy animals was regarded as bizarre. 迷
  17. Traditionally, ERVs have been named per(按照，根据) the tRNA that binds their RT enzyme and the primer binding site (PBS). Thus, HERV-K is named after(以……命名) lysine-tRNA.
  18. Each flanking viral LTR consists of a U3, R, and U5 regions in 5’ to 3’ direction. 侧翼的
  19. cations 阳离子 divalent cations 二价阳离子 anion 阴离子 ion 离子
  20. A prototype disorder is Angelman syndrome (MIM105830) in which mutations in UBE3A (MIM601623) encoding the ubiquitin ligase E3A are causative. 成为原因的
  21. In two of these cases (Isrie et al. 2012; Khalifa et al. 2013), the deletions were predicted to leave the 5′ end of ANKRD11 intact, which would lead to the production of a truncated protein missing C-terminus D-boxes. 这里intact的意思是完整不变的。这些病例中的两个，缺失导致“5‘ end是完整的”但“C端的D-boxes是截断的”。
  22. nucleus原子核，nuclei原子核（复数），注意读音，njukliai，nucleolus核仁，karyotheca核膜
  23. inclusions包含物，foci |f3usai|焦点，puctum斑点，puncta斑点（复数）|p^nkt3|
  24. Today almost every internet user is familiar with these two words irrespective (不考虑，无关，不顾) of their profession, nationality, culture, race or religion, etc.
  25. lucid明晰的，elucidate阐明
  26. Although the genome is generally thought / considered / believed to be transciptionally silent during mitosis, ….
  27. “Therefore”是带有逻辑推理的，”Thus”只是顺延下来，改用”Thus”的地方用”Therefore”是一种错误。
