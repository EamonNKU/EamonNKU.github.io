打电话50句英语

发表于2022-07-15|更新于2023-01-04|随笔杂记
|字数总计:368|阅读时长:2分钟|阅读量:|评论数:
  1. Hi, this is Mike. I’m with company ‘X’, how are you doing today?
  2. Hello, this is Mike, and I’m calling about/for_.
  3. Could I speak to Mr. ‘X’ please?
  4. Hello, is Mr. ‘X’ in?
  5. Ok, could I leave a message for Mr. ‘X’ please?
  6. I see. When would be a good time to call back?
  7. Ok. Do you know when he will be back?
  8. Hey, what’s up?
  9. Hey, how’s it going?
  10. Not much.
  11. Same old same old?
  12. Pretty good, how about you?
  13. Not bad, you?
  14. Hello? This is Mike speaking.
  15. Good morning/afternoon, this is Mike. How may I help you?
  16. May I ask, who’s calling?
  17. And which company are you with?
  18. Please hold for a moment.
  19. Ok, hang on a sec.
  20. I’m sorry, she’s busy at the moment.
  21. I’m afraid she’s not in the office right now. Can I take a message?
  22. Hello?
  23. He’s not home right now, can I take a message?
  24. Great. I’ll let him know as soon as he gets back.
  25. Sorry, I’m not interested.
  26. Can you take me off your call list please?
  27. I’m sorry. I think you’ve got the wrong number.
  28. I’m sorry, there’s no one here by that name.
  29. I’m sorry I didn’t get that. Could you say it again please?
  30. Could you spell that for me please?
  31. Ok, let me just repeat that back to you.
  32. I didn’t catch that. Could you say it again?
  33. I think my signal is bad. Could you repeat that please?
  34. We have a bad connection. Can I call you back?
  35. What time is best for you?
  36. Well, how about tomorrow?
  37. Does that work for you?
  38. Sure, no problem.
  39. I’m sorry, that doesn’t work for me.
  40. Sorry, I’ve got plans then. Could we do _ instead?
  41. I’ll have to get back to you on that. 我想想再回复你哈。
  42. It was great talking to you.
  43. Great, thanks for calling.
  44. Thanks for all of your help.
  45. You have a great day!
  46. Alright, later.
  47. You too. Have a good one.
  48. Yep! Talk to you later。
  49. Ok, see ya.
  50. Alright, bye.
