蛋白数据库

  1. 蛋白质序列数据库UniprotKB (The Universal Protein Resources Knowledge Base)
    1. Swiss-Prot: 人工注释和审核
    2. TrEMBL: 自动程序化注释

More than 95% of the protein sequences provided by UniProtKB come from the translations of coding sequences (CDS) submitted to the EMBL-Bank/GenBank/DDBJ nucleotide sequence resources (International Nucleotide Sequence Database Collaboration (INSDC)). These CDS are either generated by gene prediction programs or are experimentally proven. A protein identifier (“protein_id”) is assigned to the translated CDS and can be found in the original EMBL-Bank/Genbank/DDBJ record and in the relevant UniProtKB entry.

The translated CSD sequences are automatically transferred to the TrEMBL section of UniProtKB. The TrEMBL records can be selected for further manual annotation and then integrated into the UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot section. The “protein_id” are listed in the cross-reference part of the ‘Sequence’ section, of the UniProtKB entries (see for example P13744 ‘Translation’).

In addition to translated CDS, UniProtKB protein sequences may come from:

  • the PDB database.
  • sequences experimentally obtained by direct protein sequencing, by Edman degradation or MS/MS experiments and submitted to UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot. Only about 5% of the UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot entries contain sequence data obtained by direct protein sequencing (list of entries with the keyword ‘Direct protein sequencing’).
  • sequences scanned from the literature (i.g. PRF or other journal scan project).
  • sequences derived from gene prediction, not submitted to EMBL-Bank/GenBank/DDBJ (Ensembl, Ensembl Genomes, WormBase ParaSite or VectorBase)(1), RefSeq, CCDS, etc).
  • sequences derived from in-house gene prediction, in very specific cases.
  1. 蛋白质结构数据库PDB (Protein Data Bank)
    1. pdb格式文件可以用RasMol和PyMOL软件进行可视化

  2. 蛋白质结构分类数据库

    1. SCOP
    2. CATH

      Total number of folds is limited !
      Although the number of protein entries increases exponentially, the increasing speed of the number of new folds keeps decreasing. This indicates that the number of protein folds in nature should be limited (~2000, by estimation). The fact that many different sequences adopt a similar fold is the base of template-based protein structure prediction algorithms.

数据库规模（截止2022-05-03）

  • 240M Nucleotide sequences in GeneBank (known DNA sequences)
  • 230M protein sequences in TrEMBL (translated proteins)
  • 570K protein sequences in Swiss-Prot (Proteins with known function)
  • 190K proteins with known structure
  • ~2K different folds in nature
  • 0.55% of known protein sequences have known function
  • 0.14% of known protein sequences have known structure

基因调控数据库

ENCODE (Encyclopedia of DNA Elements, https://www.encodeproject.org/_ )

包括人、老鼠、蠕虫、苍蝇4个物种的Hi-C、ATAC-seq、RNA-seq、RIP-seq、ChIP-seq等数据

医学疾病数据库

  1. TCGA癌症数据库

  2. OMIM (Online Mendelian Inheritance in Man)数据库，在线人类孟德尔遗传数据库

    侧重于疾病表型与其致病基因之间的关联

    网址：Home - OMIM

    OMIM数据库包括1.gene entry基因条目；2.allelic variations 等位基因变异；3.gene map 基因图谱；4.phenotypic series 表型系列；5.phenotype entry 表型条目；6.clinical synopsis 临床提要；7.external links 外部链接

    数据库中的表型和基因用”MIM number”来进行索引。

    参考知乎用户 Lum Ma的文章：OMIM数据库使用指南 - 知乎 (zhihu.com)
